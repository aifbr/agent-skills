# Humanizer

Make text sound natural and specific.

## Look for

- Vague claims.
- Inflated language.
- Repeated sentence structures.
- Generic conclusions.
- Promotional tone without evidence.

## Output

- Revised text.
- Short note explaining what changed.
- Substance gaps, if the original needs stronger facts.
